## About Me

I build AI-powered products, internal tools, and full-stack web applications that are meant to work in the real world.

I bring **28+ years of software development experience** across product engineering, backend systems, frontend delivery, databases, APIs, integrations, and technical leadership. My background started long before the current AI wave, which is exactly why clients bring me in now: I do not just bolt AI onto a product. I help turn AI ideas into usable, reliable software.

Today, my work is focused on building and improving:

- AI-powered SaaS products
- workflow automations
- internal tools
- LLM-powered user workflows
- backend systems and APIs
- production-ready web applications

## How I Work

I’m most useful when a product needs more than a quick demo.

I’m comfortable joining an existing project, understanding it quickly, finding the weak points, and shipping practical improvements without unnecessary complexity. I can work hands-on across frontend, backend, database, and integrations while also helping with technical direction when needed.

What I care about most is building software that is:

- useful
- maintainable
- clear
- fast enough to feel good
- solid enough to survive real usage

AI can be powerful, but only when the surrounding product is solid. That’s the standard I work to.
